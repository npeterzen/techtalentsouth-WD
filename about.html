Early Life

Nick was born in Andover, Massachusetts on March 17, 1992.
His father is Greek American, and his mother is a Korean immigrant born in Seoul. Here he is as a young child.


At the age of 5 years old, Nick began playing the piano at the guidance of his mother and father - both of whom were musically driven.

Move To North Carolina

In the winter of 2001, Nick and his family moved to Wilson, North Carolina where he embarked on his first journey away from his home state of Massachusetts and was thrown into a melting pop of cultural diversity. The first words he learned was how to say "you all" in a single syllable - "Y'ALL!" - and the number ten now had two syllables - "TEY-AN". Nick was very confused. Until he met a young member of his neighborhood by the name of Jazzi. Jazzi taught Nick the ways of Basketball, and being a kid in rural North Carolina, and ever since then, Nick felt like he belonged. North Carolina is now his home as of today.

High School

Nick was growing up quicker than a hot bucket of molassus. By the time he became a Junior in high school, he was accepted to the North Carolina School of Science and Math where he began his training in the secret arts of Calculus, Physics, and Chemistry. Nick was very busy... ALL the damn time.

College Career

In the fall of 2010, Nick began attending the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill, where he earned a bachelors of Computer Science. This was the beginning of the adult world for Nick and he was soon to find out that life is full of surprises, hardships, successes, and failures.
